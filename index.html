How to stage this method works to help parents teach mathematics in infants and balitanya? Previously, you must understand the concepts and basic rules in this method: 1. Start as early as possible2. Be happy all the time3. Respect your child4. Teach only when you and your baby feel happy and excited5. Stop before your child wants to quit6. Return quickly7. Introducing the new object as often as possible8. Perform consistently9. Prepare materials properly and continuously10. Stop if you and your child does not have a fun time to learn Once you understand this concept, then now is the time you make props that you need to learn with your baby: 1. Dot cards: create card of manila paper or white cardboard with size 28 × 28 cm as many as 100 cards. Draw or attach each card with a circle or a red dot lights up with a diameter of 2 cm, with serial number 1 to 100. This circle must be your bunk at random, and do not forget to include the numbers on the page opposite. 2. Card Numer: draw a card from manila paper or cardboard to the size of 14 × 14 cm as many as 100 cards. Write or paste the bold figures of approximately 12-13 cm tall with bright red color, sequence of numbers 1 through 100. Step-by-step instruction: * First step: the introduction of number. Show the card with a dot, and say, "this one". Do it very quickly (in seconds) and the child should focus on the card that you show. Perform this step 3 times a day, enough only 5 cards per day. * Step two: the introduction of the equation number. Take 3 pieces of card, eg card 3, 2, and 5. Say, "two plus three equal five." Perform this step 3 times a day and enough 3 equation every day. In this step, at every interval of 2 weeks, after the accretion equations, equations also do subtraction, multiplication, and division. And remember, do not do repeat on different days and do not do the test. * Third step: solve the problem. Take 4 cards, for example, card 4, 8, 12, and 20. Say, "four plus eight equals two dozen or twenty?". Let your child think and show the answer. If your child has not been able to appoint or mention the answer, then consider the focus of his eyes. Do not force it if he is not willing or not able to answer accurately. Stay hug and give kisses full of love although the answer is wrong. * The fourth step: the introduction of numbers.
